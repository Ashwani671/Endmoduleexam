What are operations in project management?

1.Operations management deals with approaching day-to-day as well as strategic business issues systematically. Project management deals with planning, organizing, motivating, and controlling resources to achieve specific goals.


2.Project management is the process of leading the work of a team to achieve all project goals within the given constraints. 

3.This information is usually described in project documentation, created at the beginning of the development process. The primary constraints are scope, time, and budget.
